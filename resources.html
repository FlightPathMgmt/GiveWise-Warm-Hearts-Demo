Why Giving From Wealth Matters

Discover how strategic charitable giving can create meaningful impact while providing significant tax advantages. Learn why more donors are choosing to give from their accumulated wealth — such as securities, real estate, and private company shares — rather than just their annual income.

By giving from wealth, you can unlock greater resources for causes you care about while optimizing your overall financial and tax position.

What Are Donor-Advised Funds (DAFs)?

A Donor-Advised Fund (DAF) is one of the most flexible and tax-efficient ways to manage your charitable giving. Think of it as a personal charitable savings account: you make a contribution, receive an immediate tax receipt, and then recommend grants to your favorite charities over time.

Your contributions can be invested and grow tax-free, allowing you to create an even larger impact over the long term.

Why DAFs Are an Important Giving Tool

DAFs simplify the giving process by centralizing all your charitable activity in one place. They offer unique benefits, including:

  • Immediate tax deduction for the full value of your gift.
  • Flexibility to support multiple charities from a single fund.
  • Ability to donate non-cash assets like securities or crypto without paying capital gains tax.
  • Investment growth potential to increase your charitable impact over time.

How to Open a GiveWise Giving Fund

Opening your GiveWise Giving Fund is a simple, streamlined process that takes just minutes. Once established, you can immediately begin contributing assets and recommending grants to the charities you love.

  1. Visit the GiveWise platform and create your account.
  2. Choose a name for your personal or family Giving Fund.
  3. Make your initial contribution to establish the fund.
  4. Start recommending grants to your favorite charities!
