Unlocking Potential Through Digital Learning

Digital literacy is no longer a luxury—it is a necessity. At Warm Hearts, we believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by equipping underserved communities with the tools, training, and support they need to thrive in a connected world.

Our Vision

We exist to empower individuals through digital education—fostering opportunity, confidence, and connection. By providing access to technology, hands-on learning, and personalized support, we help people build the skills they need to thrive in today's connected world. At Warm Hearts Foundation, we believe digital literacy is not just about learning tools—it's about unlocking human potential, strengthening communities, and creating lasting, generational change.

Our Leadership

Tasha Raymond, Founder and Executive Director of Warm Hearts Foundation, leads a passionate team of staff, board members, and community advisors who bring deep experience and shared purpose to our work. Under her guidance, the organization is focused on building meaningful partnerships and creating programs that are both practical and people-centered.

"Digital access isn't just about technology—it's about unlocking human potential. When we invest in people, we empower entire communities to rise." – Tasha Raymond
