Strategic Giving Options

Explore personalized ways to maximize the impact of your generosity and create lasting change.

Donor-Advised Funds (DAF)

A Donor-Advised Fund allows you to make a charitable contribution, receive an immediate tax deduction, and then recommend grants from the fund over time. It's a flexible and tax-efficient way to support causes you care about while maintaining ongoing involvement in your giving.

GIVE VIA DAF

Securities & Stock Donations

Donating publicly traded stocks, bonds, or mutual funds can provide significant tax advantages. When you donate securities that have appreciated in value, you can avoid capital gains tax and receive a charitable tax receipt for the full market value of the securities.

DONATE SECURITIES

Cryptocurrency Donations

Support our mission with Bitcoin, Ethereum, or other digital currencies. Crypto donations can be one of the most tax-efficient ways to give, as you typically avoid capital gains tax on appreciated assets while receiving a charitable deduction.

DONATE CRYPTO

Legacy Giving & Bequests

Leave a lasting legacy by including Warm Hearts Foundation in your will or estate plan. A charitable bequest is a simple way to ensure your values live on and continue to make a difference for generations to come.

PLAN YOUR LEGACY

Flow-Through Shares

Flow-through shares offer one of the most tax-efficient ways to give in Canada. This strategy allows donors to receive enhanced tax benefits while supporting charitable causes. Contact us to learn more about this unique giving opportunity.

LEARN MORE

Real Estate Donations

Turn property into powerful generosity. Donating real estate—whether residential, commercial, or land—can provide substantial tax benefits while making a significant impact on our mission to bridge the digital divide.

DONATE PROPERTY
